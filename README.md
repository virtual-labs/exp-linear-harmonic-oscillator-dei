### About the Experiment 

This experiment studies the quantum behavior of a particle in a linear harmonic oscillator potential, revealing quantized energy levels and corresponding eigenfunctions. It demonstrates how quantum oscillators differ from classical ones through discrete energy spectra and wavefunction shapes.
